Explore Benthic Macrofauna with BenthixID

Our Vision

At BenthixID, we empower students, researchers, and marine enthusiasts with accessible tools to explore benthic macrofauna—key organisms in ocean health and biodiversity.

What is Benthic Macrofauna?

These are small invertebrates—like worms, mollusks, and crustaceans—living on the ocean floor. They play vital roles in nutrient cycling and sediment stability.

Why BenthixID?

Created at Cebu Technological University by Jessan Despi Moradas, BenthixID modernizes marine biology education with mobile-first tools for fieldwork and research.

  • ✔

    Simplified Species ID

    Use visual keys and descriptions for fast, accurate identification.

  • ✔

    Interactive Learning

    Quizzes and multimedia modules for hands-on marine education.

  • ✔

    Field Research Tools

    Log data, geotag locations, and annotate your findings in-app.

  • ✔

    Community Collaboration

    Share data with researchers and connect with marine explorers.
